About the quiz

According to the Global Footprint Network, "The Ecological Footprint is the only metric that compares the resource demand of individuals, governments, and businesses against Earth's capacity for biological regeneration." Essentially, based on what you consume, such as food, fossil fuels, land, and products, you can assess the sustainability of your own lifestyle!
